Our Goal

Oregon has one of the highest asthma death rates in the US nearly double the national average despite representing only 1.27% of the population. vigOR exists so Oregonians stop becoming a statistic. Monitor your air. Know your triggers. Act before an attack.

Source: CDC National Vital Statistics System (2024) · American Lung Association Asthma Trends Brief · PubMed / Oregon Health Sciences University
Our Mission

We built vigOR because the numbers are unacceptable.

Every year, 3,722 Americans die from asthma a condition that is, in most cases, entirely preventable. Oregon alone accounts for roughly 2.5% of those deaths while representing just 1.27% of the US population. That is not a statistic. That is a system failure. People are dying not because medicine doesn't exist, but because they don't have the information they need, when they need it the air quality outside their door, the trigger in their commute, the warning their body was already sending. vigOR exists to close that gap. We put real-time air data, encrypted medical records, live vitals, and an AI that never sleeps directly into the hands of every person who breathes. No waiting rooms. No clipboards. No excuses. The data is there. We just refuse to let it stay hidden.
